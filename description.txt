Nikki (from Japanese ??, "diary") enables you to share travel experiences with your friends and family. It is a standalone (Java) application that 
 * Reads photographs and GPS track files
 * Assigns each image a GPS location (based on its timestamp)
 * Allows you to annotate each photo with a title and description text
 * Exports images and GPS tracks for each day to a single, compact [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keyhole_Markup_Language KMZ file] that can be viewed in Google Earth
